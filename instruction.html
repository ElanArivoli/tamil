📖 How to Play

🎯 Goal

Combine Mei (Consonants) and Uyir (Vowels) to form Tamil letters in the matrix.

👆 Steps

1️⃣ Tap a Mei letter first (example: க்)
2️⃣ Then tap a Uyir letter (example: அ)
✅ It combines to form க் + அ = க
❌ If you tap Uyir first, it won’t work.

🧹 Clear Options

  • 🗑️ Clear All – Reset everything.
  • 🩹 Clear Previous – Undo your last move.

Click 🌐 to toggle between Tamil and English.
